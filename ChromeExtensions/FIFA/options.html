Thank you for downloading the FIFA 2014 World Cup Live Notifications app.

Here is what the app can do :
1) You will get notifications on important events in the match as the game progresses. These will be unobtrusive and will self-destruct in 10 seconds. There is nothing you need to do..
2) Optionally, You can click on the Extension icon at any time to view the current live score of an ongoing match along with the game clock timing.
3) Upcoming matches scheduled are also listed, when in the information is available.

Hope you enjoy the extension. Please share this app if you like it. Any feedback is welcome.

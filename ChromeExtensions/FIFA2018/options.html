Thank you for downloading the FIFA 2018 World Cup Live Notifications extension for Chrome.

  • Get notifications on important events in the match as the game progresses. These will be unobtrusive and will self-destruct in 10 seconds.
  • Click on the Extension icon at any time to view the most recent, live and upcoming matches of the tournament

Hope you enjoy the world cup season !!

FAQ

I use Mac and notification text seems to be chopping off
Chrome switched to native notifications by default from version 59. You can disable native notifications by going to chrome://flags/ and setting "Enable native notifications" option to "Disabled"

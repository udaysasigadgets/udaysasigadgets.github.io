Quick Stock Watch

Track your stocks with ease

  •   igHome
  •   netvibes
  •   Personal Blog

Features

  • Add a stock to your watchlist
  • Delete a stock from your watchlist
  • Sort your watchlist
  • Create and monitor your portfolio
  • View profit chart of your portfolio
  • Click a row to view more details
  • Change font size in settings
  • Click on column headers to sort
  • Click Strength to view today's performance
  • Hide the name for low-resolution monitors
  • Get news relevant to your watchlist
  • Instantly watch a chart for a stock
  • Re-order your watchlist any way you want

Add to netvibes

  • Click here to launch the Add Gadget page
  • Click on the Settings Link and enter a title of your choice
  • Enter the url in the URL field : http://udaysasigadgets.github.io/quickStockWatch.html
  • Choose the Dashboard page you would like to add this widget to and click on "Add to my dashboard"
  • You can now add your watchlist and start monitoring it

Add to igHome

  • Click here to search for the gadget
  • Click on the Add It Now button
  • You can now add your watchlist and start monitoring it

Add to any webpage

Want to add Quick Stock Watch to your personal blog/webpage? Just copy/paste the following code and you will have the gadget up and running. Each user will have their own watchlist to track

<iframe src = "http://udaysasigadgets.github.io/quickStockWatch.html" width="475" height="550" frameBorder="0" style="border:2px solid #009DD1;></iframe>

More via Real-time Traffic

Once you select your city/metro area from the preferences, the module pulls real-time traffic information and displays the complete list of freeways in your area. If you would like to limit the search to a specific freeway or a few freeways that you are interested in, please enter the freeway numbers in the Only Freeways field in the Preferences area. The module would then show the traffic information only for those freeways of your choice. 

Each freeway data has a symbol which when clicked would give you the details of real-time traffic that contains the stretch of the freeway included, number of incidents reported on the freeway etc.

Jam Factor    Clear  Clogged

Every freeway has a colored rectangle, the color of which indicates how good the traffic on that freeway is flowing. An bright green color indicates a free-flowing freeway and a bright red color indicates a clogged freeway. The rest would have colors in between. The color is determined by a factor called Jam Factor which can be anywhere between 0 and 10 (0 being the best and 10 the worst). Move your mouse pointer on the colored box to see the current Jam Factor for the freeway.

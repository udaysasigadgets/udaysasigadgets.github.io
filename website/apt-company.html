Company Profile

aptiWiz vision is to lower complexity and provide high performance, highly available, cost-effective connectivity solutions without compromising the security and control for SMEs, IoT and M2M markets.

mLINK family of products are purpose built hardware appliances (Wireless Gateway) and cloud service. mLINK Wireless Gateways and mLINK Cloud Service are built from ground up to address the challenges of the IoT (Internet of Things) and M2M (Machine to Machine) complexity, security and manageability. mLINK Wireless Gateways comes with multiple wireless radios : 4G/LTE Cellular, LoWa, ZigBee, BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) and WiFi.
